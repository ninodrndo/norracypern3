Property Description

Welcome to this exclusive luxury villa located in the sought-after area of Kyrenia in Northern Cyprus. This newly built property offers a perfect combination of modern architecture, luxurious amenities and an unbeatable location just 200 meters from a pristine sandy beach.

The villa is designed with a focus on light and space, with large panoramic windows that frame the spectacular views over the Mediterranean Sea. The open floor plan creates a natural flow between the different spaces and blurs the line between indoor and outdoor living.

The property comprises three spacious bedrooms, including a master suite with en-suite bathroom and walk-in closet. The two additional bedrooms share a well-equipped bathroom. The modern kitchen is equipped with high-quality appliances and a kitchen island that is perfect for both cooking and entertaining.

Outside awaits a private infinity pool with spectacular sea views, a spacious terrace for sunbathing and a covered outdoor area perfect for al fresco dining. The well-maintained garden is landscaped with local plants that require minimal maintenance.

The villa is equipped with underfloor heating, central air conditioning system, smart home technology and solar panels for energy efficiency. The property also includes a private garage for two cars and plenty of storage space.
