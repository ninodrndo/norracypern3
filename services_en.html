Property Purchase

We guide you through the entire process from initial interest to keys in hand.

1

Property Viewing

We arrange personal viewings of selected properties based on your wishes and requirements. For clients who cannot visit Northern Cyprus, we offer real-time virtual viewings.

2

Negotiation & Reservation

Once you've found your dream property, we help you negotiate the best price and terms. We handle the reservation process and ensure all documents are correct.

3

Due Diligence

We conduct a thorough legal review of the property to ensure there are no issues with ownership, permits, or other legal matters.

4

Contract Signing & Payment

We help you through the entire contract process and ensure all terms are favorable to you. We arrange secure payment methods and monitor the entire transaction.

5

Handover & After-service

We handle the key handover and help you with practical arrangements such as installation of electricity, water, and internet. Our service continues even after the purchase with ongoing support.

Investment Advisory

Maximize the return on your property investment with our expertise and market knowledge.

Market Analysis

We provide detailed analyses of the property market in Northern Cyprus, including trends, price developments, and future forecasts. Our deep local knowledge helps you identify the best investment opportunities.

Return Calculations

We help you calculate potential returns on various properties, taking into account factors such as rental potential, operating costs, and expected appreciation. Our detailed financial models give you a clear picture of the investment's profitability.

Investment Strategy

Based on your financial goals and risk profile, we develop a tailored investment strategy. We advise on everything from portfolio diversification to timing for buying and selling to maximize your return.

Tax Optimization

We offer advice on tax-efficient structures for your property ownership, according to both Cypriot and Swedish legislation. Our experts help you minimize the tax burden and maximize net returns.

Legal Support

Comprehensive legal advice to ensure a safe and secure property transaction.

Title Deed Review

Thorough examination of the property's title to ensure the seller has full right to sell and that there are no encumbrances or disputes.

Contract Review

Careful review of all purchase contracts and legal documents to ensure your interests are protected and all terms are clear and fair.

Building Permits & Planning

Verification of all necessary building permits and planning regulations to ensure the property meets all legal requirements and that no future issues will arise.

Tax Planning

Advice on tax-efficient structures for your property purchase, including information on double taxation agreements between Sweden and Cyprus.

Inheritance Planning

Advice on how to best structure your property ownership to facilitate future generational transfers and minimize inheritance tax.
